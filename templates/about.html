ABOUT IJHAYCLASSICS

The Journey

Renowned for his versatility, Okunade Israel Oluwasegun, professionally known as Ijhayclassics, began his musical journey by teaching himself the piano without the guidance of a tutor. The early years were filled with challenges and countless hours of trial and error, but his determination never wavered. After three years of consistent practice and self-learning, he had developed into a confident intermediate pianist.

Eight years into his piano journey, his passion for music led him to explore the guitar, which he mastered alongside music production. Through years of dedication, creativity, and continuous growth, Ijhayclassics has evolved into a versatile musician, proficient across multiple genres. Today, he works professionally as a pianist, guitarist, music producer, and music director, collaborating with numerous artists and producing countless high-quality records while helping musicians bring their creative visions to life.

My Sound

At Ijhayclassics Music, every song begins with a vision and ends with excellence. Whether you're an emerging artist, a worship leader, a band, or an established performer, my goal is to transform your ideas into records that are authentic, emotionally impactful, and professionally produced.

As a producer, musician, and arranger, I approach every project with creativity, precision, and a deep understanding of musical expression. From the first melody to the final master, I pay close attention to every detail—ensuring that each production reflects your unique sound while meeting industry standards.

My production style is deeply rooted in African music, drawing inspiration from the rich rhythms, melodies, and culture of the continent. I specialize in Afrobeats and its many variations, while also producing across a wide range of genres including Afro-fusion, Afro-pop, Highlife, Reggae, Jazz, Gospel, Contemporary Worship, R&B, Soul, and other modern African-inspired sounds.

Whether you're looking for a vibrant Afrobeat anthem, a soulful jazz arrangement, a heartfelt worship record, or a reggae groove with timeless character, I bring the musicality, technical expertise, and creative direction needed to make your project stand out.

Every production is built with originality, quality, and purpose—because great music doesn't just sound good; it tells a story, connects with people, and leaves a lasting impression.

Let's create music that inspires, moves, and lives beyond the moment.
